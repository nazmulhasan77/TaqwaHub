# Privacy Policy for TaqwaHub

Last updated: July 7, 2026

## Introduction

TaqwaHub is a Chrome extension designed to provide Islamic prayer times, Quran verses, Hadith, and productivity tools. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
- **Optional Auto Location**: If you enable auto-location, we use your device's geolocation to calculate accurate prayer times for your current location. This location data is only stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.
- **Manual Location**: You can also manually enter your city and country, which is stored locally on your device.

### Stored Data
- Settings (preferences, custom prayer times, background theme, etc.)
- Salah tracking (which prayers you've marked as completed)
- Productivity tasks and notes
- Dhikr counter data

All of this data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage or your browser's localStorage. We do not send any of this data to external servers.

## How We Use Information

Your data is used solely to provide the functionality of TaqwaHub:
- Calculate and display accurate prayer times
- Track your prayer completion
- Store your tasks and notes
- Remember your preferences

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any of your personal data to third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can:
- View your data in the extension's settings
- Delete all data using the "Reset all data" button in settings

## Children's Privacy

TaqwaHub is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
